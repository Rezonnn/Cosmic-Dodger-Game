How to Play

  • Move with Left / Right arrows or A / D.
  • Dodge all glowing asteroids — one hit costs a life.
  • Score increases the longer you survive.
  • Difficulty ramps up smoothly over time.
  • You have 3 lives. When they’re gone, your final score is recorded.

Your best score is saved locally in localStorage so it persists across sessions.
